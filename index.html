Inspiro

What is Inspiro?
Inspiro is a lightweight integration that turns ideas into visuals. With a simple text prompt, Inspiro searches for inspiring images across multiple sources (Pinterest + Unsplash) and saves curated selections directly to your Pinterest boards.

What it does

  • Searches Pinterest (with Unsplash fallback)
  • Ranks & de-duplicates images
  • Creates Pins on your chosen board
  • Optional logging to Google Sheets
